## ?? **COMPLETED MEASUREMENT TOOLS**

### **?? Basic Measurements**
1. **? Distance Measurement** (F5)
   - **Points Required**: 2 points
   - **Function**: Measure distance between two points
   - **Overlay**: Green line with distance text
   - **Keyboard**: F5

2. **? Radius Measurement** (F6)
   - **Points Required**: 3 points on circle
   - **Function**: Calculate circle radius from three points
   - **Overlay**: Blue circle with center point and radius text
   - **Keyboard**: F6

3. **? Angle Measurement** (F7)
   - **Points Required**: 3 points (vertex in middle)
   - **Function**: Measure angle at vertex
   - **Overlay**: Orange lines from vertex with angle text
   - **Keyboard**: F7

### **?? Advanced Measurements**
4. **? Two-Line Angle** (F8)
   - **Points Required**: 4 points (2 points per line)
   - **Function**: Measure angle between two lines
   - **Overlay**: Purple lines with angle text
   - **Keyboard**: F8

5. **? Polygon Area** (F9)
   - **Points Required**: 3+ points (right-click to finish)
   - **Function**: Calculate area of closed polygon
   - **Overlay**: Cyan polygon outline with area text
   - **Keyboard**: F9

### **?? Coordinate & Position Tools**
6. **? Point Coordinates** (F10)
   - **Points Required**: 1 point (single) or 2 points (difference)
   - **Function**: Display coordinates or coordinate difference
   - **Overlay**: Red crosshairs with coordinate text
   - **Keyboard**: F10

7. **? Point-to-Line Distance** (F11)
   - **Points Required**: 3 points (1 point + 2 line points)
   - **Function**: Perpendicular distance from point to line
   - **Overlay**: Brown line and point with perpendicular distance
   - **Keyboard**: F11

8. **? Arc Length** (F12)
   - **Points Required**: 3 points on arc
   - **Function**: Calculate arc length through three points
   - **Overlay**: Magenta arc segments with center and length text
   - **Keyboard**: F12
